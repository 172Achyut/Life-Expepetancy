Life Expectancy Prediction

Life expectancy is one of the most important criteria for determining the population health and henceforth the overall economic growth and sustainability of the country since healthy individuals are considered to be a productive asset for the economy and ultimately they can be able to contribute towards the development of the country in a better and more effective way. Therefore the citizens should be well aware about the average life expectancy of their country so that necessary measures can be taken at each individual level in order to improve the overall life expectancy of the nation as a whole. This is the soul idea behind the choice of the topic life expectancy prediction for our machine learning project.
